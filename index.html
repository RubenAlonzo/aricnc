About this Dog

The golden retriever is a large-sized breed of dog breed as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and upland game birds during hunting and shooting parties and were named 'retrievers' because of the ability to retrieve shot game undamaged. Golden Retrievers have an instinctive love for water and are easy to train to basic or advanced obedience standards.
